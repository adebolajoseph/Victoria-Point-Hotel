Our Services

Hotel stays, lifestyle hangouts, dining, and celebrations, all in one place.

Hotel Services

A complete, comfortable, and curated stay experience

Standard Rooms

Comfortable, clean, and well appointed rooms for solo travellers and couples. Includes king or twin beds, AC, TV, and private bathroom.

  • Queen or twin bed options
  • Air conditioning & ceiling fan
  • 40" flat screen TV
  • Private bathroom with hot water
  • Complimentary toiletries

Executive Suites

Our premium suite offering features a separate living area, premium bedding, and panoramic views, ideal for longer stays or special occasions.

  • King size premium bed
  • Living room with sofa set
  • Mini fridge & work desk
  • Premium bath amenities
  • Room service available

Reception & Concierge

Our 24-hour reception desk handles all arrivals, departures, reservations, and guest requests with warmth and efficiency.

  • 24/7 front desk service
  • Express arrival and departure
  • Tour & transport assistance
  • Local information & directions
  • Luggage handling

Housekeeping

Our professional housekeeping team ensures your room is immaculate every single day, with fresh linens, towels, and a spotless environment.

  • Daily room cleaning
  • Fresh towels & linen changes
  • Laundry service available
  • Evening service
  • On request room refresh

High Speed WiFi

Stay connected with complimentary high-speed internet access throughout the property, in rooms, the lobby, restaurant, and outdoor areas.

  • Fiber optic broadband
  • Available in all rooms
  • Public area coverage
  • Separate event hall network
  • Technical support available

Parking & Security

Enjoy peace of mind with our ample, secure parking facility monitored by CCTV and staffed security personnel around the clock.

  • Free onsite parking
  • 24/7 security patrol
  • CCTV surveillance
  • Secure gate access
  • VIP parking bays

Lifestyle & Event Services

Vibrant, fun, and tailored experiences beyond just a room

Party Hall Rental

Our spacious event hall is perfect for birthdays, hangouts, team celebrations, and casual gatherings. Fully equipped with PA system, lighting, and decor options.

  • Capacity: up to 150 guests
  • PA system & microphone
  • Ambient lighting setup
  • Event decoration support
  • Dedicated event coordinator

Snooker Lounge

Unwind and compete at our professionally maintained snooker tables in a relaxed, air conditioned lounge. Refreshments and seating for spectators included.

  • Full size snooker tables
  • Air-conditioned environment
  • Hourly table booking
  • Cue and equipment provided
  • Bar & refreshment area

Swimming Pool Access

Our outdoor pool is the perfect spot to cool off, hang out, or just soak in the sunshine. Poolside chairs, towels, and a refreshment service available.

  • Heated outdoor pool
  • Poolside lounge chairs
  • Towel & amenity service
  • Refreshment kiosk
  • Lifeguard on duty

Kitchen & Dining

Our in house restaurant serves delicious Nigerian and continental dishes from morning to night. Group catering and event menus also available on request.

  • Breakfast, lunch & dinner
  • Room service (7am to 11pm)
  • Event catering packages
  • Nigerian & continental menu
  • Fresh ingredients daily

Lounge & Hangout Space

Beyond the pool and lounge, Victoria Point has dedicated chill out areas for casual hangouts, perfect for groups who want to relax, chat, and enjoy good vibes.

  • Outdoor seating terrace
  • Shaded chill areas
  • Board games & TV lounge
  • Dedicated group spaces
  • Music & ambiance system

Event Planning & Decor

Our events team will work with you to plan, set up, and execute your event, from floral arrangements to themed decor and event day coordination.

  • Theme consultation
  • Balloon & floral decor
  • Day-of coordination
  • Vendor referral network
  • Photography coordination

Our Beginning

A Place Born from Passion

Victoria Point Hotel & Event Center was founded with a single, bold idea: that Nigerian hospitality could be both world class and warmly personal. Established in the heart of Lagos, we set out to create a space where every guest , whether checking in for a night or renting the hall for a birthday , would feel genuinely valued.

"We didn't just build a hotel. We built a destination , a place where people could stay, play, celebrate, and belong."

Over the years, we've grown from a modest guest house into a full service lifestyle destination, adding a modern event hall, a snooker lounge, an outdoor pool, and a full service kitchen. But our core belief has never changed: every guest deserves a premium experience.

Today, Victoria Point proudly serves hundreds of guests each month , from business travellers seeking a quiet retreat to families and friend-groups looking for a vibrant hangout spot.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver an exceptional, all inclusive hospitality experience that blends comfort, entertainment, and premium service , making every visit to Victoria Point feel personal, memorable, and worth repeating.

We achieve this through a dedicated team, well maintained facilities, and a deep respect for every guest who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To be the foremost lifestyle hotel and event destination in Nigeria , a brand synonymous with quality, creativity, and genuine care for the communities we serve.

We envision a future where Victoria Point is the first name every Nigerian thinks of when planning a stay, celebration, or lifestyle experience.
